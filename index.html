Our Mission

We're a North Texas-based organization created by teens and our mission is to empower women to provide the support they need. We strive to help women in our community thrive and live confidently.

We plan on donating money towards women's education and donating period products to women that may not have access to them. By joining our organization you will be able to volunteer with us to help achieve our goal of helping others!
