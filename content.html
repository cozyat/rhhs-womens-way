March Meeting Recap:
For our March series, Women’s Way held a career fair, the event provided students with the opportunity to visit various booths and ask questions about the different career paths, the challenges women face in those fields, and how they have navigated their professional journeys. It was an inspiring day that focused on empowering young women by showcasing the diverse employment opportunities available to them.

A special thank you to Mrs. Gallagher, our new sponsor, along with Mrs. Jaiswal, Mrs. Rob, Mrs. Sabonis, and Coach Holyfield for helping make this event possible!
